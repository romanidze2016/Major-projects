What is a BMR?

A BMR, or 'Basal Metabolic Rate' is the amount of energy you use throughout the day, without any exercise. Weight loss is based on the idea of a 'caloric deficit', or using more energy than you intake. As such, just by existing, you are burning off some calories! With this tool, you can find out exactly how many calories you burn a day, and thus work on creating this magical caloric deficit through a healthy diet. Additionally, as the BMR does not include any calories lost due to exercise, you can further widen the gap by working out!

Please note that it is important to not attempt any flash diets by reducing your calorie intake drastically. As a general rule of thumb, men should eat at least 1,800 calories per day whilst women should eat at least 1,200. However, please consult with your physician for more detailed information regarding your weight loss program.
