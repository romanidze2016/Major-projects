About us

Our Vision
"Freedom from Obesity"

Since the beginning, we have focused on providing our users with a relief from obesity. Whether we add new workouts, recipes, tools or motivational content to the homepage, we take great care to ensure that they will ultimately serve our users and have them one step closer to their vital goal; “freedom from obesity”.

Our Team

Our team consists of three members, Karan Ahuja, Roman Pazinich and Julian Lok. We hope to provide you with a helpful service in order to achieve your goals, whether it be helping you lose weight, helping you maintain weight, or just providing you with tools and information to make your life a little bit easier.

Privacy

Fit&Free is strongly committed towards the protection of your privacy when you are interacting with us, our content and our services.

Our goal is to provide our users with tips that will help prevent obesity and also help fight obesity. This means that we use the information provided by you to set you on the right track in order to achieve any goals you may have. We do this to improve your health and promote a healthier lifestyle tailored to you specifically and thus wish to outline our privacy policy.

The purpose of this privacy policy is to explain:
  • Application of privacy policy.
  • The kind of information Fit&Free may collect about you.
  • How you can access the information.
  • How we may use your information to contact you.

The Fit&Free Privacy Policy applies to any personal information collected by the website, whether we have asked for the information or not. We collect personal information you may provide us in order to help our users track their progress when they access their online profile. However, your information is not accessible to any other user unless you give permission for it to be so. Using this data, we can build more tailored service to you, improving your user experience.

You can access their information and privacy settings by simply logging in to the website and going to your individualised user profile. Users can also correct any information or change the information provided upon registration through this system.

If you have any issues or inquiries regarding our privacy policy, please do not hesitate to contact us here.
